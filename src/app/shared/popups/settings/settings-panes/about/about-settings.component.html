Saorsail ("Free" in Gaelic) is a web-based storefront for all apps that are free and/or open source. Built upon the incredible work of the F-Droid team, Saorsail uses the F-Droid public database to give you access to huge collection of public domain apps.

Saorsail has also been designed to make it super easy to install apps from the web, directly to your devices.
